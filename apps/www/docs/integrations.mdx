# Karrio Carriers

Karrio allow you to access a network of shipping carriers and integrate with any
additional logistics provider.

## Carriers Connections

You can connect and use services from several carriers.

## Generic Carrier

Karrio allow you to define your own carrier. This is often use to manage carriers without APIs (e.g: LTL carrier or local courier) 
under a single platform. 

And you guess right, this also means that carriers can use Karrio as their API, design their label and expose their rate sheets to customers
and modernize their infrastructure.
